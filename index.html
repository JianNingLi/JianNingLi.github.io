Jianning Li

I am currently working as a project assistant at the Institute of Computer Graphics and Vision (ICG) at Graz University of Technology (TU Graz). The focus of my current project is to seek automated solutions for cranial implant design. My research interests include machine learning, stastical shape modeling, medical image segmentation and interpretable deep learning. I received my Master's degree (Dipl.Ing) in March 2020 at TU Graz. CV available upon request. I can be reached via: jianningli.me@gmail.com.
