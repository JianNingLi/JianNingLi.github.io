Jianning Li




My research interests include computer vision, maching learning (incl. deep learning, representation learning, generative models etc), and their algorithmically non-trivial applications in medical image analysis. I am also interested in mathematics and non (fully) application-oriented research in machine learning. Don't be hesitant to drop me an email if you are interested in my work, want a collaboration and/or want to know more about me.

Job Openings

A PhD student position in medical image analysis is currently available in our group. Required skills include knowledge (theoretical and practical) of deep neural networks, especially deep generative models (GANs, VAEs, diffusion models), vision transformers and deep representation learning. Publication in major conferences (e.g., MICCAI, CVPR, ICCV) and journals (MedIA, IEEE TMI) is a plus. For more details, contact me (jianning.li AT uk-essen DOT de) with your CV (including a list of publications), research statements, and references (e.g., from former thesis advisors or supervisors).
